What is hosting?

Hosting refers to the act of storing a website on a server so that it is accessible over the internet. In other words, hosting is the service that makes it possible for individuals or organizations to make their websites available online. When you host a website, you rent server space from a hosting company and use it to store all the files, images, and other content that make up your website. The hosting company provides the necessary infrastructure to ensure that your website is always online and accessible to visitors from anywhere in the world.
